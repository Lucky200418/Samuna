Samuna

I'm Samuna an upcoming Nigerian based gospel singer, songwriter, writer and influencer.With a passion to impact the world with my talents I have a background in classical, western and afro-beat music and this has greatly influenced my singing style. My goal is to bring people closer to god through my songs and works to a dying world and especially to the younger generation.
